# Change log

## [1.1.0] - 2026-03-13

 - Add `Model.$paginate()` and `Options.$max` to configure pagination limits
   (defaults to `Infinity`).
 - Update `Cursor` to inherit from `ExplainableCursor` (previously `FindCursor`)
   to enable `AggregationSort` support.
